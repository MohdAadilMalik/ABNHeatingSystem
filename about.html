Our Story

We "Noor Enterprises", established in 2017, are one of the leading Manufacturer and Trader of Electric Heaters And Accessories, Bobbin Heater, Porcelain Heater, etc.

Our offered array is highly accepted and acknowledged in the market for their perfect finish, durability, reliability and low maintenance. Our offered range is manufacturing using top notch quality basic material and cutting edge technology in accordance to set as per international quality standards and norms.

In order to serve the enormous bulk and urgent orders of our customers, we are developing our range at our well-equipped manufacturing unit. We are backed by a team of highly experienced professionals which has enabled us to offer these supreme quality tapes as per the specific requirements of our precious clients. Our professionals work round the clock for the excellent growth of our organization.

We are able to taste the sweetness of success due to excellent guidance of our mentor "Mr. Noor Khan". He has spent long years in the industry to have rich industrial experience enabling us to understand the varied requirements of our clients.
